Browser Wars

For anyone that is currently on our website right now and reading through it, youre using a browser to visit this site. Some of the popular ones in todays day and age are Firefox, Google Chrome and Safari. However, lets take you back to how it all began...


Timeline


  • In 1992, Erwise was created which was an Internet Browser and the first to have a graphical interface.
  • In 1993, Mosaic was created and this is what popularized surfing the web.
  • In 1994, Mosaic influenced many more browsers to follow including Netscape Navigator, which became the most popular web browser at the time accounting for 90% of the web usage in 1995.

Alongside these Browser come ups, in the early 90s companies like AOL started to provide Dial-Up internet access. Dial Up is a method of connecting to the internet via a telephone line and to further explain the line was connected to a Modem and the other end was plugged into the phone jack. This was the period in which you couldnt use the telephone and the internet at the same time.
